Welcome to Black Box Productions

30 Years Experience

Our mission is to be an agent of change in the film and build environment by changing the current status quo in the industries by providing alternatives in approach and methodologies utilizing wood as base product.

To ensure all projects are undertaken with the focus on quality and cost effectiveness.

These are based on a win-win mindset, mutual trust, commitment and the sharing of risks and rewards to benefit customers directly.

Our professionals have combined several hundred person-years’ experience consulting with organisations and implementing our proposed and customer desired solutions.

Our goal is to ultimately reduce maintenance costs by introducing efficiency and the latest technology onto your premises.

Who we are

About Us

Our team is led by Gerard Gioio who has 30 years experience in the build environment both locally and internationally. He has completed 150 builts in the United States over 15 years with an average value of between $250 000 to $400 000 all completed on time.

Consist of professional carpenters; scenic specialist; metal works; landscaping design and hard landscaping with more than 30 years’ experience in each of the designation. Our team has experience in both local and international built market. Each designation is led by a team leader with strong, proven practical experience in project management skills to ensure the successful implementation of any project undertaken by Black Box
